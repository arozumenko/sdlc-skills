| Overhead type            | Without grouping | With grouping | Saved     |

**App-level state inheritance:** Each runner performed a state recovery (re-launch + re-login) despite `inherit_state: true`. Root cause: each runner subagent calls `mobile_terminate_app` at TC end, leaving the app stopped for the next runner. The `inherit_state` flag was correctly passed, but runners found the app not running and recovered gracefully. **Action item:** runner should skip `mobile_terminate_app` at TC end when the next TC in the group has `inherit_state: true` — run-lead to pass a `terminate_after` flag.
