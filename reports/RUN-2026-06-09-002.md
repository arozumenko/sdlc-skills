## TC-003 Scroll State Issue ⚠️

TC-002 scrolled to the bottom of the product list (step 6 — verify 12 items). TC-003 inherited that scroll position. Lenovo Legion (cheapest, sort ascending, normally first in grid) was off-screen at the top — the runner had to scroll up to find it, adding ~38s.

**Root cause:** `inherit_state` now correctly inherits ALL UI state including scroll position, not just auth/screen/cart.

**Action item:** TC-002 should restore scroll position to top at the end of its steps (swipe down to return to top of product list), or TC-003 notes should acknowledge this and runner should scroll up first. Alternatively the suite-planner can note "scroll_position" as a state dimension.
